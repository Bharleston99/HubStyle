Notion is a collaboration and coordination platform that integrates kanban boards, tasks, wikis and databases. It is an all-in-one workspace for notetaking, knowledge and data management, and project and task management.

I am a facilitator for the use of Notion. Notion is a very valuable tool. Here is a helpful video that explains how Notion works. I am going to teach you how to use it the most efficient way for your productivity goals.

Pricing

Notion has a four-tiered subscription model: Free, Personal, Team and Enterprise. Students and educators can get access to the Plus Plan features (with a 1-member limit) for free! Just sign up with your school email address, or change your existing email in the 'My account' tab in your existing Notion workspace.
