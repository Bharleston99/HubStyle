Notion is a collaboration platform that integrates kanban boards, tasks, wikis and databases. It is an all-in-one workspace for notetaking, knowledge and data management, and project and task management.

Pricing

Notion has a four-tiered subscription model: Free, Personal, Team and Enterprise. Students and educators can get access to the Plus Plan features (with a 1-member limit) for free! Just sign up with your school email address, or change your existing email in the 'My account' tab in Notion.
